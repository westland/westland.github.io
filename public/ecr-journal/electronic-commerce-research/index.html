Electronic Commerce Research is a peer-reviewed journal that disseminates the latest findings in all aspects of e-commerce.

Explores core enabling technologies and their societal, economic, and individual implications. Publishes theoretical and empirical research contributing to a better understanding of electronic commerce. Serves as a forum for new research in electronic commerce. Features periodic issues dedicated to a specific specialized area.
