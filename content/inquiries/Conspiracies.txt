Lizard people, Illuminati, Bilderbergs,urban legends.  Why do we devote so much of our time and attention to alledged global movements that have no impact on our daily lives, and where we would have next to zero influence?  Maybe these are constructed of the same substance as origin myths, theologies and local gossip.  Maybe they are a low consequence strategy in the competition for revenge and social status.

A good conspiricy is nothing without a kernal of truth -- a compelling mystery, an evasive explanation by authorities, and situation where truth is too complex to comprehend, so a dishonest truth is the next best choice.  

Scratch the surface of popular conspiracies, and you will find find lurcking in their darkest.  religion.  And why not?  Beliefs that are so lame, so incoherent, so obviously big lies demand their adherants have "faith" in the leaders.  And from faith come the Orwellian talents of "Big Brother", "doublethink", "Thought Police", "thoughtcrime", "Newspeak", and "2 + 2 = 5".  All fit right into scripture.
