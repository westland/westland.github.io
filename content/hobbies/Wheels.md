I've been fascinated with motorcycles since being a kid, but could only afford them after I had started my career.  I was fortunate while living in Hong Kong to have access to an F1 track in Zhuhai, where I had a paddock and race-prepared motorbike (a Yamaha R6).  I've also at times been into electric unicycles, but for the most part today I bike, along the Lakefront trails in Chicago's summers, and the Desert Ridge trails in Phoenix in winter.
